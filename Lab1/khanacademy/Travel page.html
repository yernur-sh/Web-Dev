Travel to Maldives

Where is Maldives?

The Maldives is a world-renowned tropical destination located in the Indian Ocean, consisting of 26 natural atolls and over 1,000 coral islands. It is famous for its "one island, one resort" concept, where each luxury resort occupies its own private island.

  1. Key Highlights of Maldives Tourism: Luxury Accommodations: The Maldives is the pioneer of overwater bungalows, offering direct access to the ocean from private villas.

  2. Marine Life & Diving: It is one of the world's premier destinations for scuba diving and snorkeling, featuring vibrant coral reefs, whale sharks, manta rays, and sea turtles.

  3. Geography: Most of the islands are only a few feet above sea level, making it the lowest-lying country in the world.

  4. Best Time to Visit: The peak season is during the "dry monsoon" from November to April, which offers clear skies and calm waters.

  5. Local Tourism: Since 2009, tourists can also stay in guesthouses on inhabited local islands (like Maafushi), providing a more affordable way to experience Maldivian culture.
