Rolls and sashimi

  • Time: 60 min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Fish 130 g
Rice 300 g
Vegetables 100 g

Step 1:Take the nori and sprinkle some water on it and then put it on the table.

Step 2: Cook the rice in a slow cooker, mix it with a little vinegar and spread it over the entire surface of the nori.

Step 3:Finely chop the fish and vegetables and then add them too.
